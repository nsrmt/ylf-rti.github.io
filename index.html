YLF can help you to file RTI Applications

The Right to Information Act was enacted in order to promote transparency and accountability in the working of every public authority.

Young Lawyers Forum (YLF) Kashmir is a collective​ of young lawyers and law students from Kashmir to advocate for social justice, in & out of courts. In order to do so, YLF often uses the RTI to demand transparency and accountability from government authorities and we believe that RTI serves as an important tool in our arsenal to reach the desired social & political justice.

If you have identified any issues in the society that may need to be addressed through the mechanism of RTI, we can help you.
